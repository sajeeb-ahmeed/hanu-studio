From nuke or silhouette splines to hard and motion blur mattes, we do it all.

    • Motion Capture

    Motion Capture services for animation, TV, gaming, series, or any visuals based medium and media are well offered by us. Hanu Studio's trackers clean the data for issues like jitters and obstructions. Once the clean frame is ready, we target the motion to an actor clone and retarget the motion to your character, exactly as specified. The experienced team at Hanu Studios offers total motion capture post-processing services.
