ABOUT US

Operating On-quality, Cost effective and Secure

Hanu studio is the one-stop solution to all your quality VFX and 3-D animation needs. An outsourcing company head office in Canada and production in India, we at Hanu studios help clients from across the world with highly customized solutions when it comes to Rotoscoping, Prep & Paint, Camera Match Moving, Rotomation, 3D animation and Motion Capture.

While we care enough to deliver the finest of work at a cost that fits your budget, we assure you that the privacy of everything you share with us shall be our utmost priority and never be compromised. So let’s work together to create experiences like no other.
